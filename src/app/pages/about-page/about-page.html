Company Overview

Mai-Bor Engineering & Infrastructure Ltd. is a proudly Nigerian-owned engineering and construction company headquartered in Abuja. We specialize in delivering world-class infrastructure solutions including roads, bridges, and civil engineering projects across Nigeria.

Founded with a commitment to excellence, our company combines international engineering standards with deep local expertise to address Nigeria's infrastructure challenges.

Our founder brings extensive international experience in infrastructure delivery, ensuring that Mai-Bor operates at the highest levels of technical proficiency while maintaining a strong Nigerian identity and commitment to local development.

Our Vision

"To become Nigeria's leading indigenous engineering and infrastructure company renowned for integrity, innovation, and impact."

Our Mission

"To deliver reliable, efficient, and sustainable infrastructure solutions through technical excellence, local expertise, and stakeholder collaboration."

Our Founder

Baba Bukar Isa

Director, Mai-Bor Engineering & Infrastructure Ltd.

Baba brings over a decade of international experience in infrastructure delivery, commercial management, and engineering leadership to Mai-Bor Engineering. His expertise spans multiple continents, having worked on major infrastructure projects in Europe, Africa, and the Middle East.

A strong advocate for Nigerian engineering excellence, Baba founded Mai-Bor to combine world-class engineering standards with deep local knowledge, creating infrastructure solutions that truly serve Nigerian communities.
