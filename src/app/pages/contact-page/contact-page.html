You can request a quote by filling out our contact form with details about your project, or by emailing our business development team at bids@maiborengineering.com. Please include as much detail as possible about your project requirements, timeline, and budget for the most accurate response.

Mai-Bor Engineering operates nationwide with headquarters in Abuja and regional offices in Lagos and Kano. We have successfully completed projects in all six geopolitical zones of Nigeria and are equipped to handle infrastructure projects anywhere in the country.

We specialize in road construction, bridge engineering, drainage systems, and comprehensive civil engineering works. Our expertise includes both urban and rural infrastructure projects, from small community roads to major highway networks and complex bridge structures.
